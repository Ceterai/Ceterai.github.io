# Alta Kitchenmaker

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/crafting/kitchenmaker/icon.png" alt="Alta Kitchenmaker icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Alta Kitchenmaker** is an uncommon crafting station.

An [alta](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alta) culinary workshop for crafting kitchen appliances, dishery, and cooking tools.  
The Alta Kitchenmaker is a tier 4 comprehensive culinary equipment fabrication station designed for producing everything needed in a modern alta kitchen. This versatile workshop creates kitchen appliances like icers (refrigerators), mixers, blenders, slicers, and cookdecks (stovetops), crafts dishery including plates, cups, bowls, and pots, and produces specialized kitchen tools such as knives, pizza cutters, spatulas, and measuring implements. It also creates decorative kitchen furniture and storage solutions. Essential for any alta chef, gastronomist, or home cook who takes their culinary arts seriously.
