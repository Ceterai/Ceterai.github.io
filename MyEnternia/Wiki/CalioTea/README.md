# Calio Tea

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier2/ct_calio_tea.png" alt="Calio Tea icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Calio Tea** is an uncommon drink.

A sweet and sour slightly dense tea, brewed using various alterash prime crystals. Edible ones, of course.  
Their extracts are pretty hard to get, but are very easily brewable into a typical tea.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: One might be wondering - how in Io do you make tea out of crystals? The answer is simple - you... Oh, I forgot. Unfortunate.
