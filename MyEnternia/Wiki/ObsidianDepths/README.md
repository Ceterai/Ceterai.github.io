# Obsidian Depths

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/loot/tier3.png" alt="Obsidian Depths icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Obsidian Depths** is a planetary [biome](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Biomes).

These wide dark crimson caves are made from <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/2/23/Obsidian.png" alt="Obsidian icon" loading="lazy" width="10px" height="10px"> [obsidian](https://starbounder.org/Obsidian). Their crimson colour comes from remains of some [alta](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alta) stashes, while the rest of these caves remains in the dark. What lurks inside it are vicious obsidian scorpions, darker than the night itself.
