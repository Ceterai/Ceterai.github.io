# Ava Day Gift Box

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/events/ava_day/icon.png" alt="Ava Day Gift Box icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Ava Day Gift Box** is a common decorative object.

A beautifully wrapped gift box for [Ava Day](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Tags/AvaDay). The bow sparkles with crystalline threads!  
Gift boxes like this are exchanged during Ava Day celebrations. Traditionally, they contain small tokens of appreciation - chocolates, handmade items, datamasses with messages, or small crystals. The wrapping itself is considered part of the gift, with friends often competing to create the most beautiful presentations.
