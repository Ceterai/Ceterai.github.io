# Eva Linn ★

An advanced [arknight](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Arknight) tool designed to withstand the cold and pressure in the skyes of Eva & [Astera](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Tags/Astera).
