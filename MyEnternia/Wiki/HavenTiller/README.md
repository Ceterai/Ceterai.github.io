# Haven Tiller

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/tools/till/haven_tiller/icon.png" alt="Haven Tiller icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Haven Tiller** is a rare two-handed tool.

An advanced tool to till soil. The tilled soil can then be used to grow all sorts of crops!

Can be upgraded to <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/tools/till/haven_tiller/icon_upg.png" alt="Earthcatcher ★ icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [Earthcatcher ★](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Earthcatcher) at the Weapon Upgrade Anvil.

## Ingame

Radiomessages on pickup:

- The item you just got seems to have come for alterash haven - flowery areas on alterash planets that tend to have higher levels of toxicity than surrounding nature. Although it equals to toxicity levels on most green planets, it's not a usual sight here, so I would recommend being careful.
