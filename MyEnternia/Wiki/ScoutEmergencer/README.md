# Scout Emergencer

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/weapons/ranged/alta/blaster/ct_alta_scout_blaster.png" alt="Scout Emergencer icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Scout Emergencer** is an uncommon one-handed electric unique weapon.

An emergency tool used for self defence. LMB stuns enemies in front of you, holding it launches a signal rocket.  
Produced as a neseccary survival tool, it is often used by [alta scouts](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AltaScout), [sona soldiers](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/SonaSoldier) and independent adventurers.

Can be upgraded to <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/weapons/ranged/alta/blaster/ct_alta_scout_blaster_2.png" alt="Scout Protector ★ icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> [Scout Protector ★](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/ScoutProtector) at the Weapon Upgrade Anvil.
