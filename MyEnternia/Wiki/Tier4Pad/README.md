# Tier 4 Pad

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/loot/tier4.png" alt="Tier 4 Pad icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Tier 4 Pad** is a legendary loot item.

Contains random loot from <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/ship/pod/icon.png" alt="Alta Ship Pod icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> [alta ship pods](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AltaShipPod).  
A compact blast-protected container with spaceship equipment and life packs. Its airtight construction allows it to safely store any things in a very cramped environment.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Ooh! I wonder if there's anything from [spacedrifters](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Spacedrifter) in there! I love spacedrifters! Maybe a souvenir or something? Please?

Radiomessages on pickup:

- I detected an alta loot crate in your inventory. These minipods contain random loot from related chests and pods. Altas use them for fast compact transfer, thus everything inside is packed tightly. You won't be able to put things back if you open it.
