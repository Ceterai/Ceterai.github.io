# Alta Resque Station

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/crafting/resque_station/icon.png" alt="Alta Resque Station icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Alta Resque Station** is a legendary crafting station.

An [alta](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alta) medical crafting station for emergency supplies, medicine, and protective equipment.  
The Alta Resque Station is a tier 6 emergency preparedness crafting station designed for producing medical supplies, protective gear, and survival equipment. This station creates everything from basic bandages and healing items to advanced [enhancers](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Enhancers) and [shielders](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Shielders). It also produces emergency signaling equipment like glowsticks and flares for rescue operations. Essential for any alta outpost or exploration team, the Resque Station ensures altas are always prepared for medical emergencies and hazardous situations.
