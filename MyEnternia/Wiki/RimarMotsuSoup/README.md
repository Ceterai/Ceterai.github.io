# Rimar Motsu Soup

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier3/ct_rimar_motsu.png" alt="Rimar Motsu Soup icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Rimar Motsu Soup** is a rare dish.

A tasty mix of various ingredients turned into a favorite [alta](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Tags/Alta) motsu!  
This recipe is slightly more fruit-heavy than usual, with the pallete being pretty diverse depending on its variations.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Motsus in general are a good meal to start your day off, but this one feels like you would get it any time of day.
