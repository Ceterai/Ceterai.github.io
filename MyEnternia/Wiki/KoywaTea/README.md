# Koywa Tea

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier2/ct_koywa_tea.png" alt="Koywa Tea icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Koywa Tea** is an uncommon drink.

A tea brewed from various koywa herbs. Very healthy.  
Its recipe includes petals and spices from different koywa flowers, as well as giana leaves, dryed up and mixed together. One of a few recipes to use giana leaves as an ingredient.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Appreciate a good herb mix with a roast dish, or just as it is. After properly drying and adding boiling water, even haven flowers can become a nice safe to drink tea.
