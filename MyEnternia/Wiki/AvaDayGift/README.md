# Ava Day Gift

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/loot/events/ava_day.png" alt="Ava Day Gift icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Ava Day Gift** is an uncommon loot item.

A box full of chocolates!  
Usually gifted to loved ones, this box of chocolates is a symbol of affection and care. The sweet aroma of the chocolates inside is sure to bring a smile to anyone's face. Whether it's for a special occasion or just to show someone you care, this Ava Day Gift is the perfect way to express your feelings.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: I like the ones with the tonna strips in the middle.

Radiomessages on pickup:

- I detected an alta loot crate in your inventory. These minipods contain random loot from related chests and pods. Altas use them for fast compact transfer, thus everything inside is packed tightly. You won't be able to put things back if you open it.
