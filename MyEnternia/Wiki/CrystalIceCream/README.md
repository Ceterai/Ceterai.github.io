# Crystal Ice Cream

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier2/ct_crystal_icecream.png" alt="Crystal Ice Cream icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Crystal Ice Cream** is an uncommon dish.

Crystal plants are ideal for dusting them down and making an icecream!  
Just like with most alta ice creams, isoslime plays a big role as a coolant, and koywa dough or aya dough is often used for the waffle casing.

Species descriptions:
