# Researcher Coat

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/armors/alta/clothing/researcher_coat/icon.png" alt="Researcher Coat icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Researcher Coat** is a rare chest cosmetic item.

An advanced alta researcher's coat with proper lab safety measures. Designed by Pelizhar.  
Just like other pieces of alta laboratory attire, this one provides full body coverage with optional gloves to complete the set.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: A nice middleground between official-ness and practicality.
