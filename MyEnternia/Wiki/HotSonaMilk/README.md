# Hot Sona Milk

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/events/sonaveil/ct_sona_milk_hot.png" alt="Hot Sona Milk icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Hot Sona Milk** is an uncommon drink.

Served in a cup with a handle cause it's extra hot! The snow hill on top melts pretty quickly, cooling it down.  
A warm cup of sona milk, perfect for cold days. Sona milk is known for its rich, creamy texture and subtle sweetness, making it a comforting beverage to enjoy during the chilly Sonaveil season.
