# Ion Cake

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier4/ct_ion_cake.png" alt="Ion Cake icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Ion Cake** is a legendary dish.

A bright colored cake most appropriate for parties and special occasions.  
A milk-creamy mixture with a unique taste makes this cake an entirely new thing.

## Ingame
