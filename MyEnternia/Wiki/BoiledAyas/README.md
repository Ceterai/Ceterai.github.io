# Boiled Ayas

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier1/ct_aya_boiled.png" alt="Boiled Ayas icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Boiled Ayas** is a common dish.

A bowl of boiled grinded ayas. Less sweet, but very warm and nourishing. Extra-big portion to compensate for an extra-bad day.  
Caretakers often make these for sick and unwell altas, while also adding small slices of other fruits or baking it into a pie for a group of petients.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Turn your mind off and enjoy this goodness. No chewing needed, just a spoon and some good-girl behaviour to make your caretaker happy.
