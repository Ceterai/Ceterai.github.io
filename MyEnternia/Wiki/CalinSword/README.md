# Calin Sword

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/weapons/melee/alta/light/ct_calin_sword.png" alt="Calin Sword icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Calin Sword** is an uncommon one-handed shortsword.

**WIP**. A sword made out of pure crystals. Seems like it's slowly gathering the energy around it...  
This sword was hand-made by an alta enthusiast who found a way to harden calin crystals in a way that would allow them to stay sharp and in one piece, while keeping their energy conduction capability.

Can be upgraded to <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/weapons/melee/alta/light/ct_calin_sword_2.png" alt="Crystal Blade ★ icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> [Crystal Blade ★](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/CrystalBlade) at the Weapon Upgrade Anvil.
