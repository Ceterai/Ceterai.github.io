# Enchanted Blaster

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/weapons/ranged/alta/blaster/ct_enchanted_blaster.png" alt="Enchanted Blaster icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Enchanted Blaster** is an uncommon one-handed cryogenic pistol.

This blaster can launch special charges of enchanted energy. Holding LMB will produce even weirder results.  
Judging by its casing and colors, this might have actually been just a regular toy before its "enchantment". Now instead of soft pellets it blasts out energy charges.

Can be upgraded to <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/weapons/ranged/alta/blaster/ct_enchanted_blaster_2.png" alt="Enchanted Pulser ★ icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> [Enchanted Pulser ★](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/EnchantedPulser) at the Weapon Upgrade Anvil.
