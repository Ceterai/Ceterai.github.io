# Elite Shield ★★

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wiki/Ceterai/Enternia/images/icons/ct_eds_shield-security-upgrade.png" alt="Elite Shield ★★ icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Elite Shield ★★** is a legendary shield.

A powerful fortress in the hands of an [elite guard](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/EliteGuard) that can bring anything to a halt.  
Its multilayered armor plates follow latest alta safety protocols for composite armor.

An upgrade for <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wiki/Ceterai/Enternia/images/icons/ct_eds_shield-security.png" alt="Security Shield ★ icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [Security Shield ★](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/SecurityShield).
