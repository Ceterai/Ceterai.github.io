# Prism Crippit Egg ★

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/monsters/ct_prism_crippit_egg.png" alt="Prism Crippit Egg ★ icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Prism Crippit Egg ★** is a rare one-handed throwable item.

Can be used to spawn a creature called [prism crippit](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/prismcrippit).  
These "eggs" are really just highly incubated versions of creatures, used by altas during research, often for simulation purposes, which is why they tend to be friendly to their owner.
