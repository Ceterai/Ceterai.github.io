# Alto Zych Egg

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/bugs/ct_alto_zych_egg.png" alt="Alto Zych Egg icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Alto Zych Egg** is an uncommon throwable item.

Can be used to spawn a bug called <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/bugs/alto_zych/icon.png" alt="Alto Zych icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [alto zych](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AltoZych), or simply "blue bug" in alkey.  
These "eggs" are really just highly incubated versions of creatures, used by altas during research, often for simulation purposes, which is why they tend to be friendly to their owner.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: It shimmers so beautifully in my hand! Does that mean that it likes me?! Oaaa~

## Parameters

### Small Bean

This small critter or bug can be caught with a bug net and put in a bottle.  
This will turn them into a collectable item valued by many collectionists, one of which could be yourself.
