# Alto Zych Egg

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/bugs/ct_alto_zych_egg.png" alt="Alto Zych Egg icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Alto Zych Egg** is an uncommon throwable item.

Can be used to spawn a bug called <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/bugs/alto_zych/icon.png" alt="Alto Zych icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> [alto zych](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AltoZych), or simply "blue bug" in alkey.  
These "eggs" are really just highly incubated versions of creatures, used by altas during research, often for simulation purposes, which is why they tend to be friendly to their owner.
