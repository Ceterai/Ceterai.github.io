# Warped Forest

 **Warped Forest** is a planetary [biome](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Biomes).

This land is covered in <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/throwables/ct_warped_hive.png" alt="icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [warped](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/WarpedForest) growth. Numerous <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/plants/trees/ct_nivera_tree.png" alt="Nivera icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [nivera](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Nivera) plants can be found here, as well as luminous bushes full of <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/f/f8/Glow_Fibre.png" alt="Glow Fibre icon" loading="lazy" width="15px" height="14px"> [glow fibre](https://starbounder.org/Glow_Fibre). Be careful though - this area is full of poison and wicked monsters!
