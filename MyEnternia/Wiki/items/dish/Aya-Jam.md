![ ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier1/ct_aya_jam.png) **Runeva Jam** is an uncommon lvl.3 dish.  
Can be sold for *152* <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/2/21/Pixel.png" width="12" height="16"/> [pixels](https://starbounder.org/Pixel).  
The purest jam from best baked ayas. Excellent for pancakes!  
Can be used as a substitute for aya jam.  
Usually recipes for it include mixing in bits of other common runeva ingredients, but the main one usually remains an aya.
