![ ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/critters/ct_kudok_squid_egg.png) **Kudok Squid Egg** is an uncommon lvl.3 monster_egg.  
Can be sold for *80* <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/2/21/Pixel.png" width="12" height="16"/> [pixels](https://starbounder.org/Pixel).  
Can be used to spawn a squid called [[ kudok squid|kudok-squid ]].  
These "eggs" are really just highly incubated versions of creatures, used by altas during research, often for simulation purposes, which is why they tend to be friendly to their owner.
