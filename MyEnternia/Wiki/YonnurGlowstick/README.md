# Yonnur Glowstick

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/glowsticks/yonnur.png" alt="Yonnur Glowstick icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Yonnur Glowstick** is an uncommon throwable item.

Creates a yellow bioluminescent glow when thrown. Is made from rare plant-based materials.  
Yonnur comes in different colors depending on the warped mutation.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Hopefully it's properly contained.

Radiomessages on pickup:

- Beware of the warped items you just acquired. Warped is a form of wild growth found mainly on alterash planets, that tends to spread uncontrollably and contaminate everything in its path.  
Warped things can be bioluminescent, but they also tend to be quite venomous too. I wouldn't recommend testing your luck.
