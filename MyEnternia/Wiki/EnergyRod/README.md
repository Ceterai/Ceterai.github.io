# Energy Rod

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/tools/rods/energy_rod/icon.png" alt="Energy Rod icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Energy Rod** is a legendary two-handed fishing rod.

The rod line is protected with a layer of an energy field protecting it, fit for extreme environments.
