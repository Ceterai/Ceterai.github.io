# Strizychar Egg

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/bugs/ct_strizychar_egg.png" alt="Strizychar Egg icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Strizychar Egg** is an uncommon throwable item.

Can be used to spawn a bug called <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/bugs/strizychar/icon.png" alt="Strizychar icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> [strizychar](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Strizychar), a koywa-loving horned butterfly with a prolonged body.  
These "eggs" are really just highly incubated versions of creatures, used by altas during research, often for simulation purposes, which is why they tend to be friendly to their owner.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Does it use its prolonged body to balance itself out? Very... creative?

## Parameters

### Small Bean

This small critter or bug can be caught with a bug net and put in a bottle.  
This will turn them into a collectable item valued by many collectionists, one of which could be yourself.
