# Tsay Ice Cream

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier2/ct_tsay_icecream.png" alt="Tsay Ice Cream icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Tsay Ice Cream** is an uncommon dish.

A sour icecream, how?  
The answer is simple lots of powdered tsays and ionice. Rare alta powder balls act as rare sweeteners, almost like raisins in a piece of bread.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:
