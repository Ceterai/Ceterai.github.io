# Alta Aquarium

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/crafting/aquarium/icon.png" alt="Alta Aquarium icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Alta Aquarium** is a legendary crafting station.

An [alta](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alta) aquatic research station for studying marine life, fishing, and cultivating water crops.  
The Alta Aquarium is a tier 6 comprehensive aquatic biology station dedicated to studying and preserving aquatic lifeforms. This station produces fishing equipment including rods, reels, and lures, creates spawn eggs for marine creatures like jellies and squids, and provides tools for cultivating aquatic plants. It features specialized equipment for malacology (study of mollusks), jellyology (study of jellyfish), and general marine biology. The station also crafts aquarium display containers for showcasing collected specimens. Essential for any alta interested in underwater exploration and aquatic ecosystems.
