# Barren Ion Core

 **Barren Ion Core** is a planetary [biome](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Biomes).

Narrow caves close to the <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/loot/biome/ct_ion_core_loot.png" alt="icon" width="16" height="16"/> [ion core](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AlterashPrime#ion-core) of the planet, full of <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/2/23/Obsidian.png" alt="Obsidian icon" loading="lazy" width="10px" height="10px"/> [obsidian](https://starbounder.org/Obsidian) and other hard materials and minerals. Here you can find tier 5 ores like <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/7/78/Aegisalt_Ore.png" alt="Aegisalt icon" loading="lazy" width="16px" height="16px"/> [aegisalt ore](https://starbounder.org/Aegisalt_Ore), <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/b/b8/Ferozium_Ore.png" alt="Ferozium icon" loading="lazy" width="16px" height="16px"/> [ferozium ore](https://starbounder.org/Ferozium_Ore) and <img src="https://starbounder.org/mediawiki/images/3/3a/Violium_Ore.png" alt="Violium icon" loading="lazy" width="16px" height="16px"/> [violium ore](https://starbounder.org/Violium_Ore).
