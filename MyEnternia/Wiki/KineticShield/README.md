# Kinetic Shield

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/shields/ct_kinetic_shield.png" alt="Kinetic Shield icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Kinetic Shield** is a rare electric shield.

This directional energy shield multiplies the impact and returns it to your enemies, launching them into the air.  
It's powered by and projected from a special tiny wrist device that connects to your armor.

Can be upgraded to <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/shields/ct_kinetic_shield.png" alt="Energy Blockade ★ icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> [Energy Blockade ★](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/EnergyBlockade) at the Weapon Upgrade Anvil.

## Parameters

- Shield Health: 28
- Min time: 0.2s
- Cooldown: 0.45s
- Knockback: 64
