# Dreamer's Flower Sapling ★

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/farmables/alta/liquid/dream/sapling/icon.png" alt="Dreamer's Flower Sapling ★ icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Dreamer's Flower Sapling ★** is an uncommon plantable item.

A proper sapling that takes less space and has increased and more consistent speed of growth stages. Requires to be at least 50% in a liquid.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: A dreamer's flower... better not to fall asleep and to gather its sap.
