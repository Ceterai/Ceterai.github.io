# Alta Clubhouse Brochure

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/codex/alta/paper/calin.png" alt="Alta Clubhouse Brochure icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Alta Clubhouse Brochure** is a rare codex entry.

A simple piece of sturdy paper with bright pictures. It's a visit card of a city clubhouse.  
Thankfully, despite being a blatant advertisement, the material is fully recyclable.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Ooh, I should probably go here sometime!.. Wherever this is.
