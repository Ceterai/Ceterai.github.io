# Cooked Tsay

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier1/ct_tsay_cooked.png" alt="Cooked Tsay icon" loading="lazy" height="16px" width="auto" /> **Cooked Tsay** is a common dish.

Unlike its raw version, cooked tsay tastes mostly like meat. ...Unless you add some spices! Tasty!  
Adding spices and marinades to cooked tsays is a common practice among altas, who are not very fond of the taste of meat in general.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Goes very well as a side-dish to something bitter or spicy, gives it a sort of balance in flavor.
