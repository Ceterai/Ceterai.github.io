# Maito Tea

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier2/ct_maito_tea.png" alt="Maito Tea icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Maito Tea** is an uncommon drink.

A very calming tea mixture used to relax and concentrate.  
This tea doesn't contain many spices, but is high on tonna milk and ayaka extract, making it perfect for all those tiresome and lazy days.

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: This is often called a comfort food by many altas, because of how just plain nice and mello it is.
