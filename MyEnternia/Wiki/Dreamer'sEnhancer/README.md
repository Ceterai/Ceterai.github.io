# Dreamer's Enhancer

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/armors/alta/tier6/dreamer/enhancer/icon.png" alt="Dreamer's Enhancer icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Dreamer's Enhancer** is a legendary enviro protection pack.

A worn device that enhances a dreamer's abilities. Has an augment slot.

## Ingame

Radiomessages on pickup:

- You've got a hold of a level 6+ alta back armor piece. Aside from providing special effects, these usually have an augment slot, allowing you to enhance them with an augment of your choice.
