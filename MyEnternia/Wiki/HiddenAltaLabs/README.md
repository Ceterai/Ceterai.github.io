# Hidden Alta Labs

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/loot/tier3.png" alt="Hidden Alta Labs icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Hidden Alta Labs** is a planetary [biome](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Biomes).

Due to frequent kaiter raids on <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/interface/bookmarks/icons/ct_alterash_prime_planet.png" alt="icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [alterash prime](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AlterashPrime) planets, [altas](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alta) began hiding their labs and facilities here - in <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/interface/bookmarks/icons/ct_alterash_prime_planet.png" alt="icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [esetera](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AlterashPrime#esetera). You can expect a high level of security and lots of <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/drones/watcher.png" alt="Watcher Drone icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [watcher drones](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/WatcherDrone) and <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/spawners/droids/spider.png" alt="Spider Droid icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [spider droids](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/SpiderDroid).
