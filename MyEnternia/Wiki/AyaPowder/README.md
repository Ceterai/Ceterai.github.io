# Aya Powder

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/other/ct_aya_powder.png" alt="Aya Powder icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Aya Powder** is a common cooking ingredient.

Some finely grinded dehydrated <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/produce/ct_aya.png" alt="Aya icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> [ayas](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Aya).  
Often used either in cooking, or in energy filters, depending on the type of grind and different mixes or aya variations, it also makes for a good edible dye. Read more about this: <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/codex/alta/paper/ayaka.png" alt="Ayaka Gardener's Notes icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> [Ayaka Gardener's Notes](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/AyakaGardener'sNotes)

## Ingame

Species descriptions:

- Alta: Eating raw aya powder isn't very healthy, because it doesn't have any moisture in it. So, to counter that, you can roll it up in some <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/generic/food/tier1/ct_iso_jam.png" alt="Izo Jam icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> [izo jam](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/IzoJam) into a small tasty ball of goodness. My friend calls them **maris**. And yeah, you can thank me later.
