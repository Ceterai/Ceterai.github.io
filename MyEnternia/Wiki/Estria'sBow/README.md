# Estria's Bow

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/items/active/alta/toys/bows/love/icon.png" alt="Estria's Bow icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Estria's Bow** is an uncommon two-handed bow.

Arrows loosed from this bow provoke unbridled passion.  
Named after [Estria](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Niverisk), a nivera sentia known for her passionate nature, this bow shoots heart-shaped arrows that inspire feelings of love and connection. It's a popular [Ava Day](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Tags/AvaDay) gift for those who want to playfully express their affection! The bow's elegant pink design captures the warm, romantic spirit of the holiday.
