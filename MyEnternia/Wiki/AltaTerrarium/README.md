# Alta Terrarium

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/crafting/terrarium/icon.png" alt="Alta Terrarium icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Alta Terrarium** is an uncommon crafting station.

An [alta](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alta) geology and archaeology station for studying rocks, excavating fossils, and preserving specimens.  
The Alta Terrarium is a tier 4 geological research station dedicated to the study of minerals, crystals, and ancient life. This comprehensive workshop produces archaeological tools like brushes and excavation equipment, creates display cases for rock samples and [fossils](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Tags/Fossil), and processes various geological materials. It features crystal analysis equipment for studying <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/interface/bookmarks/icons/ct_alterash_planet.png" alt="icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> [alterash](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Alterash) crystals, fossil preparation tools for displaying ancient remains, and terrariums for preserving mineral specimens. Essential for any alta geologist or paleontologist.
