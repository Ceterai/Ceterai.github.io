# Christmas Poster

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/events/sonaveil/ct_christmas_poster.png" alt="Christmas Poster icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"/> **Christmas Poster** is a common decorative object.

A festive poster celebrating Christmas - a human holiday very similar to Sonaveil!  
Both holidays emphasize warmth, gathering with loved ones, and gift-giving during the coldest season. Some altas who are interested in human culture display these posters alongside traditional Sonaveil decorations, finding common ground in the universal desire to stay warm together when it's cold outside.
