# Ava Day Poster

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/special/events/ava_day/ct_ava_day_poster.png" alt="Ava Day Poster icon" loading="lazy" width="auto" height="16px"> **Ava Day Poster** is a common decorative object.

A cheerful poster celebrating [Ava Day](https://ceterai.github.io/MyEnternia/Wiki/Tags/AvaDay) - the alta friendship holiday where friends exchange gifts and appreciation!  
This poster features warm, friendly designs with hearts and stars, promoting the spirit of connection and appreciation. Ava Day is when altas celebrate all types of bonds - between friends, colleagues, caretakers, and loved ones. It's a day to show gratitude for the people who make life meaningful.
