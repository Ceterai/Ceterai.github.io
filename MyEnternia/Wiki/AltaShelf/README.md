# Alta Shelf

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Ceterai/Enternia/main/objects/alta/basic/shelf/icon.png" alt="Alta Shelf icon" loading="lazy" height=16px width="auto" /> **Alta Shelf** is a common decorative object.

A simplistic titanium shelf with small crystal markings. **Is a platform. Can be placed on walls, as well as the ground (in form of a small table).**  
Most basic alta furniture is designed to be simple, lightweight, and, most importantly, easy to clean and disassemble. The goal is to be comfortable and portable at the same time, while also being easy to recycle if needed.
